Resource Fish Changes
- Full recipe overhaul for breeding and infusing, breeding recipes allow you to get from base fish (water, lava, dirt, stone, wood)
    to every other type of resource fish through a series of breeding steps and new breeding items.
    Infusion recipes allow you to infuse a fish with a resource to change its type directly.
    This helps modpack makers control the availability of certain fish types and restrict in ways they see fit.
- Added Colored Fish for all base colors, produce dyes
- Added Glowstone, Quartz Fish
- Added fish should be obtainable through breeding and infusing.
